LEGACY OF INNOVATION

Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the company has established itself as one of the most prestigious sports car manufacturers in the world. With a rich racing heritage spanning over seven decades, Porsche's engineering excellence has been proven on racetracks worldwide.

The first Porsche 356 was produced in 1948, marking the beginning of a legendary lineage that would culminate in the iconic 911, introduced in 1963 and still in production today.

Today, Porsche continues to push boundaries with innovative technologies, electrification, and sustainable mobility solutions while staying true to its core values of performance, quality, and driving pleasure.

About Porsche
